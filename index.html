--- title: Weekend hackups layout: base ---

Weekend hackups are a monthly excuse to make something cool. They are about creating instead of axe-sharpening. Hackups are inspired by rapid prototyping, rough cuts, and the 80-20 rule: it should only take 20% of the time to implement 80% of functionality, so let's crank the 80 in a weekend and make some shit already.

It's simple

  1. Hackups start at 8PM on Fridays, end on Sunday at 5PM, when a show-off and recap is held. All participants must show off.

  2. Participants must enter the hackup with an idea of what they want to make.

  3. Participants must do their best to complete an implementation of that idea.

  4. Drinking beer is allowed. Pizza is encouraged.

Ideas can be anything, often code-related, but you've gotta have something to show for the weekend.
